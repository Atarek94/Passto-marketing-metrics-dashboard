## Overview

This interactive Tableau dashboard was built at **Passto** (FCA-regulated international money transfer app) to provide the Marketing and Operations teams with a unified view of business performance across the full customer lifecycle.

The dashboard is structured around the **AARRR Pirate Metrics framework** — a widely used growth analytics model that tracks customers from first touch through to referral, giving leadership actionable insight at every stage of the funnel.

## AARRR Framework

The dashboard is organised into 6 sections, each mapping to a stage in the AARRR growth funnel:

| # | Section | AARRR Stage | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Activation & Conversion** | Acquisition + Activation | Tracks new user sign-ups, onboarding completion, and first transfer conversion rates |
| 2 | **Operation Efficiency** | Activation | Monitors transfer processing times, success rates, and operational KPIs |
| 3 | **Retention & Engagement** | Retention | Measures returning users, transfer frequency, and long-term engagement trends |
| 4 | **Customer Satisfaction** | Revenue | Captures satisfaction scores, support ticket trends, and NPS indicators |
| 5 | **Referral & Virality** | Referral | Analyses referral programme performance, viral coefficient, and friend invites |
| 6 | **Customer Demographics** | All stages | Breaks down the user base by geography, nationality, and segment |

---

## Key Business Questions Answered

- How many new users are converting from sign-up to first transfer?
- Where are users dropping off in the onboarding funnel?
- What is the monthly and yearly retention rate?
- Which referral channels are driving the most high-value customers?
- What does the customer demographic profile look like across regions?
- How satisfied are customers and how is it trending over time?

## Dashboard Features

- **Navigation menu** — cover page with clickable buttons routing to each section
- **Cross-dashboard filtering** — interactive filters by date range, country, and flat type
- **YoY comparisons** — 2022 vs 2023 trend analysis across all key metrics
- **KPI summary cards** — headline numbers visible at a glance on each section
- **Drill-down capability** — click into charts for granular breakdowns
